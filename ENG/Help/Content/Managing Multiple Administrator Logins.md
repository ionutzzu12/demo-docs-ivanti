Multiple admin portal sessions are supported so the administrator can view different pages of the portal at the same time. If you are an administrator, you can view your last login date to help you keep track of multiple logins.

## View the last administrator login

**Procedure**

1. Click the **Account** \`
2. View the **Last logged in:** entry.
