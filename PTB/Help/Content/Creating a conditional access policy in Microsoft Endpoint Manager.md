Procedure

1. Log in to Microsoft Endpoint Manager [**https://endpoint.microsoft.com**](https://endpoint.microsoft.com/).
2. In the Microsoft Endpoint Manager admin center page, go to Home > Endpoint Security > Conditional Access.
3. Click Policies and then click + New Policy.

   ![](Resources/Images/MSEM_01_AddnewPol.png)
4. Enter the Name of the conditional access policy.

   ![](Resources/Images/MSEM_02_AddnewPol.png)
5. In Assignments, click to assign the policy to users and groups.

   ![](Resources/Images/MSEM_03_AddnewPol.png)
6. Click Cloud apps or actions and then click Select. Search for and select the apps required to be protected.

   ![](Resources/Images/MSEM_04_AddnewPol.png)
7. Click Conditions and then click Device Platform. Select the appropriate device platforms.

   ![](Resources/Images/MSEM_05_AddnewPol.png)
8. In the New conditional access policy page > Access controls, click Grant and make the access and block selections.

   ![](Resources/Images/MSEM_06_AddnewPol.png)
9. To enable the new policy, click On.

   ![](Resources/Images/MSEM_07_AddnewPol.png)
10. Click Create.
