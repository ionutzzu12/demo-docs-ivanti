After you retire a device, you can delete it. Deleting it removes it from all pages. You can delete a device only if its status is Retired, Retire Pending, Wiped, or Wipe Pending.

**Procedure**

1. Go to **Devices > Devices**. The devices list appear on the screen.
2. Select the device you want to delete.
3. Click **Actions**.
4. Under the **Common** section, select the **Delete Devices** option. A pop up appears on the screen.
5. Select the "I understand that Delete cannot be reversed" option, and then click **Delete device(s)**.
6. Alternatively, you can delete a device by following the steps below:
   - Go to **Devices** > **Devices**.
   - Under the **User Display Name** column, click any link. The **Device Details** page appears on the screen. Click **Device Details**. A pop up appears on the screen, and if you acknowledge the warning and click **OK**, the specific device will be deleted.

## Deleting multiple devices

You can delete multiple devices by selecting all the devices you want to delete.

**Procedure**

1. Go to **Devices > Devices**.
2. Select the devices from the available device list.
3. Click **Actions** tab, under the **Common** heading select the **Delete Devices** option.
4. On the **Delete Device(s)** window:
   1. Select the **I understand that Delete cannot be reversed** check box.
   2. Click on **Delete valid device(s)**, to delete the selected devices.
